Chicken With Green Bean Fries

Description

Here is a simple yet delicious meal that can be prepped in under 30 minutes. Quick and easy low carb meal perfect for keto. This is a staple in my keto meal plans when I need something quick and easy.

Ingredients

  • 2 (1 lb) chicken breasts 8 oz (230 g) each
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp taco seasoning (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 6 oz. fresh green beans or wax beans
  • 2 tbsp light olive oil or coconut oil (flavorless)

Steps

  1. To speed up the cooking process, slice the chicken breasts horizontally and lengthwise into thin cutlets.
  2. Season both sides of the chicken with salt, ground black pepper, and seasoning of choice.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the oil or butter to the skillet when hot. Carefully place the chicken breasts in the skillet and cook for about 4 minutes on each side. The chicken is ready to serve when the inside is no longer pink.
  4. Wash and trim the green beans. Pat them dry with a paper towel (to avoid oil splatters).
  5. Heat a large skillet with oil over medium-high heat.
  6. Add the green beans to the pan and fry for about 5 minutes. They should retain their fresh, natural crunch!
  7. Place the green beans on a plate with a paper towel to absorb excess oil. Season with salt before serving.
